A little bit about myself

Hi there, I am Yunior, a passionate Front End Developer. The web has always seemed very interesting to me, and while working on my Bachelors in Computer Science, I decided that I wanted to be a Web Developer. Now, I am 100% dedicated to creating websites, helping customers and learning up-to-date modern technologies. When I am not in development mode, I enjoy watching movies with my family, go fishing or otherwise, I lay back and relax.
